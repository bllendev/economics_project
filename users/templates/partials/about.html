About Kalibre!

How to Use Kalibre?
  • Create an account or log in if you already have one.
  • Add email addresses to which you'd like to send your ebooks in 'my emails'.
  • Click 'kalibre' in the upper-left corner to return to the book search page.
  • Find your book and send it with a simple click!
Send Books Directly to Your Kindle:
  • Login to your Amazon account.
  • Go to 'Manage Your Content and Devices'.
  • Under 'Preferences', find 'Personal Document Settings'.
  • Add bllendev@gmail.com to 'Approved Personal Document E-mail List'.
